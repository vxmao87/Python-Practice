A bird flutters its wings, and the past is blown away by the wind.
No one sleeps, because everyone can't stay asleep to begin with.
Kids play at the park, filling the area with joyful noise. The whole
place felt like a utopia to Jacky, who wanted no more than to live
a comfortable life without much thought as to what to do when there's
trouble. Little did he know that his world would be turned upside-down
when the earthquake of the century struck.
